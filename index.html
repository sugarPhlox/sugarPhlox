About Me

Born in Massachusetts, I grew up surrounded with love and support by my family to pursue anything I enjoyed in the world, and took full advantage of that. In high school I enjoyed finer arts and photography, and took that with me to Salem State University (based in Salem, MA), where I received my degree in Graphic Design (after starting as a Biology major... really).

In summer 2013, I looked at my cat Buddy and said 'We're moving to Burlington, VT', and a month later I moved, and soon after started learning front end development, which has brought me here today.

Other than my love for various forms of art, I do enjoy a good life in VT. My passions include my vehicles (a 2009 Subaru WRX and a motorcycle), good cooking, my fantastic cat, and learning something new every day.
